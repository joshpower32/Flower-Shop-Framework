About Bloom & Co.

We’re a small, family-run florist studio creating fresh arrangements by hand every morning. From a single stem to a full event, every order is made with locally grown flowers and delivered with care across Hamilton and the surrounding area.

Have a question? Reach out any time — we love helping you find exactly the right blooms.

Areas

Hamilton, Dundas, Ancaster, Stoney Creek, Waterdown. Outside these areas? Just ask.

Fees & timing

Flat $12 delivery, or free over $75. Same-day on orders placed before 1pm, Monday–Saturday.

Pickup

Prefer to pick up? Choose “Pickup” at checkout and collect from our studio, free of charge.
